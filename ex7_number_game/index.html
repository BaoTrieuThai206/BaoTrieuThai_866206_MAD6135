NUMBER GAME

Arrange the numbers from 1 to 15 in ascending order with the blank tile at the end.

How to Play: Click on a number tile to move it into the empty space. Tiles can only move horizontally or vertically, not diagonally.

Winning: You win when all the tiles are in order from 1 to 15, and the blank tile is at the bottom right.

Have fun and see how quickly you can solve the puzzle! Remember, fewer moves are better.

Click the "Start" button to shuffle the tiles and start a new game anytime.
